What is Adachi's TDS Ranker?

Adachi's TDS Ranker, also known as the TDS Tier List Maker, is the best free tool for creating, sharing, and exporting custom tower tier lists for the Roblox game Tower Defense Simulator. Easily rank all towers, filter by category, and export your tier list as an image or wiki code for the TDS Wiki. Whether you're a new player or a TDS veteran, our tier list editor helps you organize and showcase your favorite towers.

This tier list creator supports all current towers, including starter, intermediate, advanced, golden, hardcore, and exclusive towers. Use the gallery to browse every tower, put them into your preferred tier, and share your rankings with friends or the TDS community. Stay up to date with the latest meta and balance changes by revisiting and updating your tier lists anytime.

Looking for the most accurate and up-to-date TDS tier list? Our tool is trusted by thousands of players and is regularly updated with new towers and features. Bookmark this page and join the Tower Defense Simulator Wiki community for more guides, strategies, and resources!

This tool is an unofficial creation and is not an official product of Paradoxum Games
