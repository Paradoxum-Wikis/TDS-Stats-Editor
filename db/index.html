Help
Searching

You can use the search bar at the top of the page to find towers by its name, description, or author. Just start typing and the results will appear automatically.

Filtering

There are filter checkboxes on the sidebar to show or hide specific tower types:

  • New - Completely custom concepts that isn't in the game
  • Rework - Complete redesign of an existing tower already in the game
  • Rebalance - Purely statistics adjustments to towers already in the game
  • Unverified - Submissions that aren't verified or just not up to standard
Sorting

On the same sidebar, there are sort options which you could use to sort towers by:

  • Wiki Listing - Original order as listed on the wiki
  • Alphabet (Tower) - Sort alphabetically by tower name
  • Alphabet (User) - Sort alphabetically by author name
  • Time - Sort by upload date

Pro Tip: Click a sort option again to reverse the sort order!

View Options

Toggle between Grid and List view using the buttons in the top right corner.

Why is my tower unverified?
  • The database utilize a verification system to help curb spam and inappropriate content
  • If a submission isn't working properly (broken JSON, image, etc.), it won't be verified.
  • Submissions are manually reviewed and will have its unverified tag removed once, well, verified
What are featured towers?
  • If a tower submission is considered to be of great quality, this tag will be added to it
Where do I get JSONs?
  • The TDS Statistics Edtior, our main site, has an option to export JSONs
  • You could write JSONs yourself, though any bugs caused by that won't be fixed
What are blog posts?
  • Because blog posts will inherently always be more detailed than the statistic editor, we have included an option to link your towers to a blog
  • However, blog posts are expected to be of a much higher standard than the usual JSONs
Why isn't my image showing up?
  • Make sure the image is displayed properly in the upload preview first before uploading
  • Providers like Discord will automatically delete your image link after 24 hours
  • You may upload your images to the TDS wiki, however, the image quality will be worse
Need more help? Visit the TDS Wiki Discord for more information and support!
Guidelines

Please follow these guidelines when uploading towers to keep the database great and awesome.

Account Requirements
  • You must have a account to upload towers
    • Because of this, you need to be at least 13 years old to upload towers
    • 16 and older if you're in California and the EEA (excluding the UK)
    • Of course, you are free to browse the database without an account
  • Do not try and impersonate anyone, be yourself
Content Guidelines
  • Tower names should be names, don't make them too long
  • Descriptions are more laxed, describe the towers however you want
  • Make sure to choose the appropriate tag (New, Rework, or Rebalance)
  • Provide an image if possible
Content Restrictions
  • No inappropriate or offensive content
  • Do not copy someone else's work verbatim and claim it as yours
  • Do not flood or spam the database with uploads

Grandfather Clause: Any submissions made before the creation of the guidelines are exempt from them and thus verified. This means that the first 7 towers submitted during the BETA phase should not be used as a reference for standards.
